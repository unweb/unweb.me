Okeanos Cloud Computing UI Design.

Okeanos is an Infrastracture as a Service solution provided by the Greek Research and Technology Network to the Greek Academic and Research Community.

The cloud computing services are based on Ganeti and Python. Unweb.me created the UI of the solution, with Python and Django, plus lots of Javascript and CSS. We aso assisted GRNET since the early beginnings of the project and took part on major discussions regarding the service infrastructure.

Okeanos GUI enables the creation and management of Virtual Machines through wizards and an intuitive environment. Okeanos users can create, destroy, reboot or shutdown virtual machines, connect to them through a vnc console, enable firewall, get statistics and perform a series of other actions.
